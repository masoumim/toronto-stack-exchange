Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Q:

What should I bring?

Any device you code on (laptop, tablet, or even a steam deck) and maybe a project you're working on too!

Q:

What types of devs can join?

Any type of developer! Web, mobile, software, firmware, front-end, back-end, full-stack and everything in between.

Q:

Is the group beginner friendly?

Absolutely! You don't even need to have written a single line of code to join the group. First-timers and people just starting to learn programming are more than welcome.

Q:

Is it free to join?

TSE is 100% free to join. We only ask that you purchase a drink or snack from our host venue.
